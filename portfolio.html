💡 Afiya — Wellness App (Concept → Prototype)

Role: Product & Business Analyst • Deliverables: research, journeys, wireframes, functional specs

Problem

Users want simple, actionable wellness guidance—not fragmented insights across multiple apps and metrics.

What I did

  • Ran user research with 15+ participants and competitive analysis of 5 wellness platforms.
  • Designed user journeys, wireframes, and functional specifications for a personalized recommendation engine (“Today’s Focus”).
  • Facilitated iterative feedback sessions with a 20-user beta, refining usability and increasing daily habit completion by 30%.
